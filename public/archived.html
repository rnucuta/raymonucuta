1st PVC Air Rifle - 2014

This is the first iteration of my PVC air rifles. It has a "bolt action" which I made out of vinyl and lubricated with WD-40.

2ndPVC Air Rifle - 2015

This is the first iteration of my PVC air rifles. It also has the same design as the last rifle except it has a larger air capacity and it has an electronic trigger. Click here for a video of me making it.

1st Motorized Bike

I built this using an old bike I had and a kit I bought on Ebay. This was a definite mistake because the parts were so cheap. However, at the same time, I think in the end it was worth it because it was a valuable learning experience. I got to take apart an engine and see how a flywheel, clutch, and throttle worked. Click here for a slideshow of this project.
